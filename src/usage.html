What is Algebruh and how to use it?

Algebruh is a quick solver for Algebra related questions. It operates on the principle of simplicity: drop your question, get your answer!

  • Drop the image. Drag and drop the image into the dropbox, straight from the question site: no need to save or copy it!
  • Await answer fetching. The image will be automatically searched through the answer database.
  • Get your answer! After a few moments, the answer to the question will appear on the screen.

That's it. Go ahead and give it a try!
